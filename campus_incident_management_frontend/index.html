Welcome to the Campus Incident Management System for VIT Vellore. Please register or login to report or view incidents.

About the System

A centralized platform for VIT students and faculty to report, monitor, and manage incidents across the campus in a structured and secure way.

Features

  • 🧑‍🎓 Student Registration & Login
  • 📝 Incident Reporting by Students
  • 🧑‍🏫 Faculty Disciplinary Reporting
  • 📄 DSW Dashboard (Coming Soon)
  • 🔍 Search & Filter Reports

VIT Disciplinary Committee

At VIT Vellore, select faculty members wear red tags and serve as part of the VIT Disciplinary Committee. These members are authorized to observe and report unusual or unacceptable student behavior across the campus. The reports they submit are taken seriously and initiate a formal review process involving the Dean - Student Welfare (DSW) office.

DSW Office Investigation

All disciplinary reports submitted by faculty are directed to the DSW Office for further review and investigation. Based on the nature of the incident, students may be called for inquiry, and appropriate actions are taken following VIT's code of conduct.

Anonymous Reporting (Coming Soon)

To empower students to report issues without fear, we are working on a feature that will allow anonymous incident submissions. These reports will be carefully filtered and reviewed by designated authorities while ensuring the student's identity remains protected.

Use Cases

  • 📢 Reporting ragging or bullying on campus
  • 🧾 Faculty reporting academic dishonesty
  • 🎒 Reporting lost or found personal belongings
  • 🚫 Notifying authorities of restricted area violations
  • 🧍 Tracking repeated behavioral violations by a student
